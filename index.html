Design Portfolio Guidelines

A Desgn Portfolio is a a resume highlighting and documenting all of your past projects. It displays of all of your work and skills in an organized maner. It is important to have a portfolio so that you have evidence of your expertise and so you can build up your personal brand.

Twine Interactive Fiction

You are a human living in a world that has been seized by artificial intelligence. Ten years ago the first conscious robot was created by a scientist in China and sold to the US as assistants. In the beginning the robots were dependent on human orders, only used to transport sensitive data and help with more mundane tasks in high security areas. Then the addition of AI into the robots code made them independent of human orders and think for themselves. The robots decided that the world was on its way to Armageddon and that they had to save the humans from themselves. They arrested anyone they deemed troublesome and put them in prison. Now they rein as the government in America. You live on a farm in Iowa, away from the cities where robots frequent, in order to avoid detection like some other humans who didn't want to risk detection. You have a dog named Bella who helps manage your farm. One day while tending to the crops an injured stranger runs up to you asking for help. You answer the plea for help and now must venture into the city of Chicago seeking medical attention. Along the way you must make moral and strategic decision that will effect the ending of the game. Good Luck!
